Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

MovaTalk respects your privacy.

Voice translation, conversation translation, camera translation and photo translation are designed to be processed on your device.

MovaTalk does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

No audio, text, images, or translations are sent to external servers by the app.

If optional online features are added in the future, users will be clearly informed and asked for consent before any data is transmitted.

Permissions

The app may request access to the microphone, speech recognition, camera, and photo library only to provide translation features.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@movatalk.com
